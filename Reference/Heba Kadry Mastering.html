Heba Kadry is a mastering engineer based in Brooklyn, NY. Artists she’s worked with include: Björk, Sade, Gorillaz, Bon Iver, John Cale, Slowdive, Ryuichi Sakamoto, Brockhampton, Kim Deal, Sufjan Stevens, Deerhunter, Beach House, Hayley Williams, Cate Le Bon, Alex G, Miguel, Battles, John Maus, serpentwithfeet, Blonde Redhead, Yaeji, Daniel Caesar, Animal Collective, Nicolás Jaar, Diamanda Galás, Lightning Bolt, Mk.gee, Cass McCombs, Japanese Breakfast and The Mars Volta to name a few. In addition to mastering original soundtracks for films such as “Nosferatu”, “The Northman”, ” Jackie” (Oscar-nominated for best original score in 2017), “Midsommar”, “The Lighthouse”, “Candyman”, “Father Mother Sister Brother” and “L’étranger”. Check out her discography and bio for expanded credits.
